Belem

Our confectionery is named after the Belém Tower which was built in the early sixteenth century in Lisbon, Portugal.

Its construction was to commemorate Vasco da Gama's expeditions. However, it’s also believed that the Tower was to aid in defending the harbour and also to be a type of customs outpost.

This elegant construction has become one of the symbols of the city, a memorial to Portuguese power as it was during the Age of Discovery.
