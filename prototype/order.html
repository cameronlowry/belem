Cake Orders

Please note that we do NOT take orders over the phone. To place an order you will need to come into the shop. The earlier you come in the better but we suggest at least two to three days before the event. If you are looking for something other than a simple vanilla or chocolate sponge cake then you will need to give us more notice. This includes orders for things like fruit cake and carrot cake.

Wedding cake orders should be done at least two weeks in advance and even more if your ceremony is on a long weekend or over Christmas or other busy holidays.

Having said that, if you are late or in need of an emergency cake we will do our best to help you. We generally have extra sponge and fruit with which we fulfil last minute orders.

We also satisfy the demand for daily sweet and savoury items with a full selection of pies, confectionery and bread. Come in and try our cake slices, cupcakes, pastries, biscuits, scones, raisin buns and more.

Although we have a large range of designs for our birthday and wedding cakes, we can also make something different if you so choose. If you have an idea for a cake that is not in our album please come in and show us your proposal.
